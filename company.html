Build Your Alternative Drinking Lifestyle

Like everyone we love travelling! While on a holiday, you discover new beverages that have low sugar content and provide an alternative to sugar-loaded fizzy drinks. Sadly, the availability of alternate beverages is very limited in India.

In many countries around the world, people have options of less sugary drinks to quench their thirst in addition to providing a dose of vitamins. Keeping this in mind, our company Beltek Canadian Water Ltd started working on the idea of introducing a smart hydration alternative in India, which tastes good as well as provides a healthier option for our family.

Wild-Water™ is a water based drink, with :

  • 100% natural flavours
  • 100% natural colours
  • 0% preservatives
  • Low Sugar

Wild-Water™ :

  • Provides a dose of vitamins
  • Is prepared with finest ingredients from USA
  • Contains mouth-watering flavours that shine through
  • Maintains International Quality Standards
  • Brought to you by a Company having almost 2 decades of experience in beverage industry

    Each Wild-Water flavour has a unique profile specifically formulated to create a delicious beverage suitable for Indian palate. The concoction has been formed with minimum amount of pure cane sugar, as an alternative to super-sweet juice or fizzy drinks. And we are proud of it!
